My Favorite Recipe

  1. Classic Grilled Steak Recipe:
    • 2 boneless ribeye steaks
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  2. Instructions:
    1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Brush both sides of the steaks with olive oil and season with minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
    3. Place the steaks on the hot grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the time to your desired doneness.
    4. During the last minute of cooking, brush the steaks with Worcestershire sauce for added flavor.
    5. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.
    6. Serve the grilled steaks with your favorite sides and enjoy!
